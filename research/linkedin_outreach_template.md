## Cold Call Script (If Phone Numbers Available)

**Opening**:
"Hi, my name is [Your Name]. I'm calling regarding academy trial opportunities. Is this a good time for a brief conversation?"

**If Yes**:
"Great! I'm a [Age]-year-old [Position] player currently with [Club]. I've been following [Club Name]'s academy and I'm very interested in being evaluated for your program."

**Be Ready to Answer**:
1. Why this specific academy?
2. What's your playing background?
3. Can you send materials today?
4. When are you available?

**Closing**:
"Thank you so much for your time. I'll send my highlight reel to [email] right away. Is there a specific person I should follow up with in a week?"

## Timing Strategy

### **Best Times to Reach Out**:
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM EST
- **Avoid**: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends
- **Peak Season**: August-September (pre-season planning)
- **Secondary**: January-February (mid-season adjustments)

### **Follow-Up Schedule**:
- Day 0: Initial contact
- Day 7: First follow-up
- Day 14: Second follow-up (if no response)
- Day 21: Final follow-up with new content
- Day 30: Move to different scout at same club

---

## Red Flags to Avoid

❌ Don't send mass generic messages  
❌ Don't be pushy or entitled  
❌ Don't send poor-quality videos  
❌ Don't lie about stats or achievements  
❌ Don't message late at night  
❌ Don't follow up more than 3 times  

✅ **DO**: Be professional, concise, and provide value  
✅ **DO**: Show genuine interest in the specific club  
✅ **DO**: Have materials ready before reaching out  
✅ **DO**: Thank them regardless of response  

---

## Response Rate Expectations

- **Connection Request Acceptance**: 30-50%
- **Message Reply Rate**: 10-25%
- **Actual Trial Invitation**: 2-5%

**Strategy**: Contact 50+ scouts to get 5-10 meaningful conversations to get 1-2 trial opportunities.

## How to Get Scouted

### Direct Paths:
1. **Email Academy Directors** - Professional highlight reel + stats
2. **Attend Open Tryouts** - Most clubs host 1-2 per year
3. **Submit Player Profile** - Via club websites
4. **Play in Showcases** - MLS Next, ECNL, GA Cup
5. **LinkedIn Outreach** - Connect with scouts, share highlights

### Required Materials:
- ✅ Professional highlight video (2-3 minutes)
- ✅ Full game footage
- ✅ Stats sheet (goals, assists, position)
- ✅ Academic transcript
- ✅ References from current coaches
- ✅ Birth certificate (age verification)
